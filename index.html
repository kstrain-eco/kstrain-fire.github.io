Current Research

I am a PhD student in the Ecology Evolution and Conservation Biology program. My research interests lie at the intersection of invasion ecology, disturbance ecology, and conservation. I am interested in understanding how wildfire, elevational gradients, and climate change affect plant community assembly within an ecosystem.
